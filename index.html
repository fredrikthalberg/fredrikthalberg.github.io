About me

I’m a Swedish composer who produce and write music for television, commercials, trailers, games and other visual media. My ideas often start by the piano. I’m a pianist but my influences come from all kind of music styles and instruments. My music has been featured in Swedish TV shows such as:

  • Efterlyst (Strix Television)
  • Jul med Ernst (OTW Television)
  • Halv åtta hos mig (ITV Studios)
  • Det okända (Nordisk Film)
  • Sveriges mästerkock (Meter Television)
  • 112 på liv och död (Titan Television)
  • Arga snickaren (Skare)
  • Bonde söker fru (FremantleMedia Sverige)
  • Kimura Talent (Kimura.se)
  • Battle of Botnia (ICON Studios)

Games: Rudolph's Challenge (MRP Group)
